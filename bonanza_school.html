VIVO Energy Ghana Bonanza School Challenge

Research shows that learning in general is enhanced through the medium of play. Students enjoy playing games and they learn faster by participating and embracing the concept of practical play. When fun activities are included in the teaching process, the rate of understanding of complex concepts is greatly increased.

Daffodils Games and Publishing Limited (Daffodils GP) and VIVO Energy Ghana believe in this and offers a challenging and educational learning experiences for these young minds through the BONANZA card game product. We are bringing a unique and exciting product closer to pupils and students at the pre-tertiary level.

The BONANZA card game employs basic arithmetic skills to provide endless hours of exciting challenge which will pit school teams first against each other and then against teams from other schools to find a single reigning Bonanza Champion.

The challenge will take part in three main stages:

  • Introduction; presentation and activity time with the students
  • Intra-school competition; where teams compete against each other within the school; and
  • Inter-school challenges; teams compete with teams from other schools.
