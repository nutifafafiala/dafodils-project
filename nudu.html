Bonanza Card Game
You are here: Home / Products / Bonanza Card Game

Nudu is a simple board game with a simple objective… Fulfilment in life. Nudu recreates lifes journey to fulfilment in an exciting board game that people of all ages will enjoy. Take on the character of either Kelli or Kobbo and steer your game pieces through life from employment to fulfillment.


How to Play
Nudu is played among a maximum of 4 players.The objective of the game is for all a players game tokens to reach “fulfilment” the first to do this wins.
